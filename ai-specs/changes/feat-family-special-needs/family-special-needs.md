# Family Special Needs Feature Implementation

## Overview

The Family Special Needs feature is designed to provide additional support and resources for families with special needs. This feature will include various tools and services to assist families in managing their unique challenges effectively.

## Key Features

Special needs:

- Mobility assistance
- Adapted living arrangements
- Meal restrictions: gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, allergies, etc.

## Implementation Plan

1. Family members will be able to specify their special needs in their profiles.
