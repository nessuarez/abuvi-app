# First version of the mock for the 50th anniversary of Abuvi

## Objective

Create a mock website for the 50th anniversary of Abuvi for demonstration purposes. The mock should include a landing page with information about the anniversary, a section for uploading photos, and a contact form. The design should be simple and visually appealing, reflecting the spirit of Abuvi.

Take as an example this website: <https://2f3dc0e1-2d12-4472-91e0-62a095a00437-00-1ta7f294a59di.riker.replit.dev/aniversario>

## Features

1. **Landing Page**: A welcoming page that introduces visitors to the 50th anniversary of Abuvi, including a brief history and significance of the event.
2. **Upload Section**: A section where users can upload their photos, videos or audios related to Abuvi, with a simple form for submitting their name and photo.
3. **Timeline**: A timeline showcasing the key milestones and events in Abuvi's history over the past 50 years.
4. **Photo Gallery**: A gallery to display the uploaded photos, allowing visitors to browse through them and see the memories shared by the community.
5. **Contact Form**: A form for visitors to get in touch with the organizers, including fields for name, email, and message.
